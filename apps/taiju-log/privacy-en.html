Privacy Policy

App: きょうの体重 (Today's Weight) / Effective date: June 29, 2026

“きょうの体重 (Today's Weight)” (the “App”) is provided by misoclub (“we,” “us,” or “our”). We respect the privacy of our users and establish this Privacy Policy (this “Policy”) as follows.

1. Information We Collect and Where It Is Stored

The information you record in the App (weight and body fat percentage; height and target weight; meal and exercise notes; short memos; and settings such as notifications and themes) is stored only on your device. It is not transmitted to or collected by our servers. No account registration or login is required. Values such as BMI and averages are calculated on your device from the above data for display purposes and are not collected separately.

Accordingly, this data is erased when you delete the App. When switching devices, please use the App’s “Backup (export) / Restore (import)” function (you are responsible for managing the exported file).

2. Integration with Health Apps

The App includes a feature to sync your weight and body fat percentage with your device’s health platform (Android: Health Connect / iOS: Apple Health). This integration operates only when you explicitly turn it on in the settings screen.

  • When the integration is on, the App reads and writes (two-way sync) your weight and body fat percentage data with Health Connect / Apple Health on your device.
  • This exchange of data is completed within your device (the OS health platform) and is not transmitted to or collected by our servers.
  • You can turn the integration off at any time in the settings. You may also delete from within the App the data that the App wrote to Health Connect / Apple Health (we do not delete data written by other apps).

Because health information is sensitive, we do not transmit it externally or provide it to any third party.

3. Notifications

The App’s notifications (such as reminders to record your data) are local notifications generated on your device. We do not collect personal information for the purpose of notifications.

4. Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob, provided by Google, to deliver advertisements. For ad delivery and performance measurement, AdMob may obtain and use an advertising identifier (Advertising ID) and information about your device and usage. The handling of such information is subject to Google’s Privacy Policy.

  • Google’s Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • You can configure ad personalization and reset your advertising identifier from your device settings.

If ads are hidden within the App (where such a feature is offered), no ads will be displayed.

5. Provision to Third Parties

Because we do not collect the information you record on our servers, we do not provide it to any third party. The handling of advertising-related information is subject to the policy of Google AdMob (Google LLC) described above.

6. Permissions Used by the App

The App may use the following permissions to provide its features.

  • Notifications: for record reminders (local notifications)
  • Internet connection: for ad delivery
  • Access to health data (weight and body fat percentage): for syncing when the health integration is turned on

7. Children’s Privacy

The App does not collect information targeted at any specific age group. If the App is used by a child, please use it under the supervision of a guardian.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Policy may be revised as necessary. If there are significant changes, we will announce them on this page. The revised content takes effect at the time it is posted on this page.
